How it works


Students

The teacher downloads the questions by topic and grade or validates the questions made by his students and exchange questions between the groups to answer them.
 

Teachers

The students can create the questions so other groups can answer them while answering the other group's questions or already made questions downloaded by the teacher in a competitive game to get to the top of the mountain.
